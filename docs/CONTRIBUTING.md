# Contributing a Seedance 2.5 Case

This collection publishes evidence-backed production workflows, not prompt-shaped marketing copy. A useful submission lets another creator inspect the direction, verify the model, watch the result, and adapt the method.

## Evidence Bundle

Open the **Submit a Prompt** issue and include:

- a concise workflow title and one-sentence production purpose;
- the complete prompt actually used, including reference roles, camera, timing, sound, and continuity instructions where relevant;
- the canonical public source and original creator attribution;
- a direct video or stable source page where the result can be played;
- the input mode: text-to-video, image-to-video, multimodal reference, editing, or extension;
- explicit evidence that the result was produced with Seedance 2.5;
- one primary category from `docs/CATEGORY_TAXONOMY.md`.

## Model Scope

The launch collection accepts only records explicitly identified as `seedance-2.5` by their source. Do not relabel Seedance 2.0, Seedance 1.5, or another video model because its prompt might also work in a future release.

## Editorial Review

1. **Traceability**: source, creator, result, and model claim can be verified.
2. **Prompt integrity**: verbatim text is preserved, or any completion is labeled honestly.
3. **Production value**: the case teaches a reusable decision about references, blocking, camera, sound, continuity, or editing.
4. **Video evidence**: the media proves motion or timing; a disconnected still is insufficient.
5. **Uniqueness**: source, normalized prompt, video URL, and preview do not duplicate an existing case.
6. **Rights**: attribution remains visible and removal requests take priority.

Engagement can help discovery, but it cannot replace evidence.

## Corrections and Removal

Creators and rights holders may open an issue with the affected record and requested action. Verified attribution corrections and removal requests receive priority over ordinary additions.
