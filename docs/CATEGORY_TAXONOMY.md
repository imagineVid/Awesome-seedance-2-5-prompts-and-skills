# Seedance 2.5 Workflow Taxonomy

Each case receives one primary workflow. Choose the category that captures the main production problem being solved, not every technique visible in the clip.

## Multimodal Reference Direction

Use when the prompt's central lesson is how separate images, clips, audio, or other references are assigned to identity, composition, movement, sound, or style.

## Long-Take Blocking & Camera Paths

Use for continuous movement through space, coordinated subject blocking, moving-camera geography, reveal timing, or transitions that depend on a planned camera path.

## Dialogue, Foley & Music Timing

Use when dialogue, lip movement, Foley, ambience, music, or a timed audio cue carries the scene and must align with visible beats.

## Narrative Continuity & Character Performance

Use for multi-beat acting, emotional progression, identity preservation, wardrobe stability, motivation, or spatial continuity across a narrative sequence.

## Product, Fashion & Campaign Motion

Use for advertising and editorial work where product geometry, garment details, branding, offer timing, or campaign presentation must remain controlled.

## Video Editing, Extension & Restyling

Use when an existing clip is extended or changed. The submission should state both the requested edit and the continuity that must survive it.
